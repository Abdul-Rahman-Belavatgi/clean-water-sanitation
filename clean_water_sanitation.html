About This Portal MBMC Clean Water & Sanitation Initiative

Problem Statement

In metropolitan areas like Mira Bhayander, the quality of water supplied by municipal corporations is often inconsistent and, at times, unsafe. Irregular water treatment and distribution can result in contamination, posing significant health risks.

Poorly treated water can lead to waterborne diseases such as cholera, typhoid, and gastrointestinal infections. There is a pressing need for effective monitoring systems and real-time alert mechanisms.

📌 UNSDG Goal 6 — Clean Water and Sanitation

Our Solution

This portal enables citizens to file water complaints with image proof, track resolution in real time, and hold MBMC accountable through transparent public dashboards.

  • 📋 Ward-wise complaint filing with image upload
  • 📊 Real-time dashboards for citizens and admins
  • 🎯 Priority-based auto-filtering for admins
  • 💬 Community comment system
  • ✅ Transparent resolution tracking
  • 📈 Monthly and ward-wise trend analytics
